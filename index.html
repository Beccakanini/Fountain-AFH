Our Homes

At Fountain Adult Family Homes, we take pride in providing exceptional, person-centered care in a comfortable and home-like setting. Both of our locations—Fountain 1 in Tacoma and Fountain 2 in Lakewood—are thoughtfully designed to support the daily needs and well-being of our residents.

We offer a wide range of services including personal care, assistance with daily living activities, nutritious home-cooked meals, and individualized support plans tailored to each resident. Our homes are also conveniently located near major hospitals like Tacoma General and St. Joseph, allowing for easy access to specialized medical care whenever needed.

What Makes Us Special

At Fountain Adult Family Home, we take pride in creating a welcoming and joyful environment for both male and female residents. What truly makes us special is the vibrant sense of community and the variety of activities that bring our home to life each day.

From friendly games of bean bags and lively board games to baking delicious cupcakes and cookies together, every moment is an opportunity for connection and fun. Our residents also enjoy light exercises and range-of-motion activities to stay active, along with creative pastimes like puzzles, art books, and card games that stimulate the mind and inspire creativity.

Our Services

We provide personalized care—including daily assistance, nutritious meals, housekeeping, and medical support—designed to ensure comfort, dignity, and a true sense of home for every resident.

Personal Care

Our personal care services include compassionate, respectful support with daily needs such as bathing, grooming, dressing, and mobility.

Daily Living Assistance

From help with housekeeping, meals, and medication reminders to gentle support with routines and activities, we ensure comfort, independence, and peace of mind every day.

Nutritious Meals

We provide nutritious, home-cooked meals thoughtfully prepared to meet each resident’s dietary needs and preferences.

Housekeeping & Laundry

From regular room tidying to fresh linens and clothing care, we handle every detail so residents can relax and feel truly at home.

24/7 Supervision

Our dedicated caregivers are always on-site and attentive, offering peace of mind through continuous support and compassionate care day and night.

Medical Supervision

With support from visiting doctors, a Nurse Delegator, and on-call specialists, we ensure every resident’s medical needs are met promptly and professionally.

Optional Services

  • Medication Management

    Professional help with administering and remembering medications for consistent wellness.

  • Transportation Assistance

    Safe, reliable help getting to appointments, errands, or community activities.

  • Social & Recreational Activities

Meet Our Founder

Visionary • Leader • Caregiver

My name is Alice, and I have been proudly serving in this field for over 10 years. Our journey began with Fountain 1 Adult Family Home in Tacoma, which has provided compassionate care for nearly a decade. In March 2024, we expanded our mission by opening Fountain 2 Adult Family Home in Lakewood.

At both homes, we are an all-female team, dedicated to creating a warm, safe, and nurturing environment for our residents. We typically have two experienced female caregivers on site, ensuring personalized attention and consistent support throughout the day.

My passion for this work comes from a deeply personal place—a lifelong desire to help others and enhance their quality of life. Caregiving, for me, is more than a profession; it’s a calling. The most rewarding part of what we do is seeing the difference we make every day in the lives of our residents and their families.

At Fountain Homes, we believe in treating everyone with dignity, respect, and genuine care—just like family.
